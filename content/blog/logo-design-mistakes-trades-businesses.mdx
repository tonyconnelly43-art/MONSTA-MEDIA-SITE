A logo is often the first impression a homeowner has of your business. For home service companies, it
needs to work small on a business card and huge on the side of a truck.

## 1. Too Many Colors

Stick to two or three colors max. It keeps printing costs down and makes your brand instantly recognizable.

## 2. Fonts That Don't Scale

Thin, decorative fonts disappear at a distance. Choose bold, simple type that reads clearly on a van wrap
or a yard sign.

## 3. No Brand Guide

Without a style guide, every print shop and sign vendor makes their own call on your colors and spacing.
That inconsistency adds up fast.
