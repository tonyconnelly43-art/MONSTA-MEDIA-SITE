Your work van spends more hours parked in front of customers' houses than almost any other piece of your
marketing. A well-designed wrap turns every job site, every gas station stop, and every red light into a
free ad for your business.

## What Makes a Van Wrap Work

A wrap that actually generates calls needs three things: a legible logo, a phone number readable from
across the street, and a color scheme that matches the rest of your brand &mdash; uniforms, signage, and
your website.

## Common Mistakes We See

Too much text, low-contrast colors, and phone numbers that are unreadable at a glance are the most common
reasons a van wrap fails to bring in leads. Bold, simple, and consistent wins every time.
